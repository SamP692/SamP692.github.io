Sports—namely baseball and soccer—are a lifelong obsession of mine. A huge part of this passion comes from the stories and mysterious patterns that the numbers of a game can tell you. Conversely, the inexplicable nature of sports, and the endless behaviors that numbers fail to explain time and time again contribute to just as much of the intrigue for me.
