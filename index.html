The YADG Game

One of the most exciting games ever released on the web, the YADG Game is a sports gambling simulator. The goal of the game is to grow your wealth from $50,000 to one million dollars by placing bets on different games. Every virtual "day" there are ten games to bet on. Each game has betting odds for each team, as well as your scouting team's prediction for each team's likelihood to win.

Update: Release 1.1 updates both betting odd and scouting odd generation to reflect greater realism.
